Interview with Yuan Lee

Interviewer
Yuan, thank you for taking the time to speak with us today. Your work has been gaining a lot of attention lately. Can you tell us about your creative process and what drives your artistic vision?
Yuan Lee
Thank you for having me. My creative process is really about finding the intersection between technology and human emotion. I'm fascinated by how digital tools can amplify our most intimate experiences. I often start with a feeling or memory, then explore how to translate that into something visual and interactive.
Interviewer
Your recent series explores themes of identity and belonging. What inspired you to focus on these particular themes?
Yuan Lee
Growing up between cultures, I've always felt like I was navigating multiple worlds simultaneously. There's this constant negotiation of identity that happens when you don't quite fit into any single category. I wanted to create work that speaks to that experience, but also celebrates the richness that comes from existing in those in-between spaces.
Interviewer
What's next for you? Are there any new projects or directions you're excited to explore?
Yuan Lee
I'm working on a collaborative project that combines virtual reality with traditional storytelling techniques. I'm really interested in how immersive technology can create deeper empathy and understanding between people. The goal is to create experiences that help us see the world through different perspectives, literally and figuratively.
